<h2>Functional Specifications</h2>

    <p>Electronics for the active antenna must be designed with the following functional specifications</p>

<ul>
    <li>The design must be compatible with an active whip antenna of 50cm length and 5mm diamter</li>
    <li>The active antenna must receive power and communicate via the same 50 Ohm coax cable with SMA connectors which is connected to the interface</li>
    <li>The supply voltage must be less than 10V DC.
    <li>The input of the active antenna should be protected against ESD discharge</li>
    <li>The electronics should withstand temperatures between -25 and 70 degrees Celcius</li>
    <li>The active antenna must contain an amplifier</li>
</ul>
