<h2>Performance Specifications</h2>

<p>Electronics for the active antenna must be designed with the following performance specifications</p>
<ul>
    <li>The power consumption should be less than 0.25W</li>
    <li>The -3dB freguency range of the active antenna should be equal to 10kHz to 30MHz</li>
    <li>The electrical field strength at the antenna input should be equivalent to 0.45 Vrms m^-1</li>
    <li>The inaccuracy of the gain at frequencies between 100kHz and 1MHz should be less than 20%</li> 
    <li>The antenna-referred quivalent noise spectral density limits can be obtained by linear interpolation of the following list <ul>
            <li>10kHz        200nVm^-1 Hz^-0.5 /li>
            <li>100kHz       20nVm^-1 Hz^-0.5 /li>
            <li>1MHz         10nVm^-1 Hz^-0.5 /li>
            <li>100MHz       10nVm^-1 Hz^-0.5 /li>
        </ul>
    </li>
</ul>
